## 时间表达法
1. 整点： 点钟+（o'clock)
   - eight o'clock
   - ten o'clock
2. 半点:half past+点钟
   - half past nine
   - half past eleven
3. 分钟不过半：分钟+past+点钟
   - twenty past eight
   - ten past five
   - a quarter past four
4. 分钟过半：剩余分钟数+to+下一点钟
   - five to ten
   - twenty to eight
   - a quarter to eleven

- 美式简单表达方法：
  小时+分钟
  - one twenty
  - one thirty
  - one fifty five
